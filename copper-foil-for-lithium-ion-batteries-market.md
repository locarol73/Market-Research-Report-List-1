<p><p>The copper foil market for lithium-ion batteries encompasses two primary types: electrolytic copper foil and rolled copper foil. Electrolytic copper foil, produced through an electrochemical process, offers superior conductivity and is commonly used in high-performance batteries. In contrast, rolled copper foil is created by mechanically rolling copper into thin sheets, making it cost-effective for applications requiring moderate performance. Both types are essential for enhancing battery efficiency and energy density, catering to the growing demand in electric vehicles and portable electronics.</p></p>
