<p><p>The carotid artery stenosis stent system market includes various types of stents designed to treat narrowed carotid arteries. Bare metal stents provide structural support without drug coatings. Stent grafts combine stenting and grafting, offering a reinforced option for repair. Drug-coated stents are infused with medication to prevent restenosis by inhibiting cell growth. Degradable metal vascular stents dissolve over time, allowing natural artery healing while providing temporary support. Each type serves specific clinical needs in managing carotid artery stenosis.</p></p>
