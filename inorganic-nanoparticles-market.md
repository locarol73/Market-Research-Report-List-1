<p><p>The inorganic nanoparticles market comprises two primary types: powder and dispersion liquid. Powder nanoparticles are solid particles utilized for various applications, including catalysts, pigments, and additives, benefiting from ease of storage and transport. In contrast, dispersion liquids contain nanoparticles suspended in a liquid medium, offering enhanced stability and uniformity for applications like coatings, inks, and pharmaceuticals.
