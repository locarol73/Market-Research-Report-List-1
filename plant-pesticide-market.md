<p><p>The plant pesticide market comprises various types, including phytotoxins, phytogenous insect hormones, and others. Phytotoxins are naturally occurring toxic substances in plants used to deter pests. Phytogenous insect hormones, derived from plant sources, regulate insect growth and reproduction, serving as biological control agents. Other categories include biocontrol agents, microbial pesticides, and essential oils, which offer alternative pest management solutions. Together, these types represent a diverse portfolio aimed at sustainable agriculture and environmentally friendly pest control practices.</p></p>
