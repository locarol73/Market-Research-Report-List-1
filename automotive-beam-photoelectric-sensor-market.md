<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Beam (Photoelectric) Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-Beam Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Reflective Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Diffuse Photoelectric Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive beam (photoelectric) sensor market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Through-Beam Photoelectric Sensors** utilize a transmitter and receiver placed opposite each other, detecting objects that interrupt the beam.</p><p>2. **Reflective Photoelectric Sensors** feature a single unit that sends and receives signals by reflecting them off nearby surfaces, suitable for shorter distances.</p><p>3. **Diffuse Photoelectric Sensors** rely on the reflection of light from the target itself to detect objects, ideal for detecting varying surface conditions and distances.</p></p>
