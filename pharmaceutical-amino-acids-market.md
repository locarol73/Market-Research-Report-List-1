<p><p>The pharmaceutical amino acids market is categorized into two main types: non-essential amino acids and essential amino acids. Non-essential amino acids can be synthesized by the human body and play roles in protein synthesis and metabolic processes. In contrast, essential amino acids cannot be produced by the body and must be obtained through diet or supplements; they are crucial for various physiological functions and cellular health. Both types are important for developing therapeutic products and nutritional supplements.</p></p>
