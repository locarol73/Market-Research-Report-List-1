<p><strong>The UAV ESCs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BEC ESCs</li><li>OPTO ESCs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the UAV ESC (Electronic Speed Controller) market, two primary types are prominent: BEC ESCs and OPTO ESCs. BEC (Battery Eliminator Circuit) ESCs integrate a voltage regulator, providing power to the UAV's receiver and servos, streamlining the setup. OPTO ESCs, on the other hand, do not include a built-in power supply for the receiver, relying on an external source for power. This distinction affects weight, efficiency, and application suitability, catering to varying UAV performance and power requirements.</p></p>
