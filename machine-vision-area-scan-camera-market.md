<p><ul><li>Line Scan Camera</li><li>Area Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Machine Vision camera market comprises primarily two types: Line Scan Cameras and Area Scan Cameras. Line Scan Cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for inspecting long objects or continuous processes, such as in web or textile applications. In contrast, Area Scan Cameras capture the entire frame in one shot, making them suitable for applications requiring high-resolution images of stationary or moving objects, such as quality control and automation in manufacturing environments. Both types play crucial roles in industrial automation and visual inspection.</p></p>
