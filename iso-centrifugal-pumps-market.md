<p><ul><li>Horizontal ISO Centrifugal Pumps</li><li>Vertical ISO Centrifugal Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ISO centrifugal pumps market is divided into two main types: horizontal ISO centrifugal pumps and vertical ISO centrifugal pumps. Horizontal pumps feature a horizontal shaft orientation, making them ideal for large fluid volumes and applications requiring high flow rates. They are commonly used in water supply and industrial processes. In contrast, vertical ISO centrifugal pumps have a vertical shaft design, suitable for applications with limited space and deeper installations, such as groundwater extraction and drainage systems. Both types ensure efficient fluid transport in various industries.</p></p>
