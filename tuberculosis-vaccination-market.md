<p><p>The Tuberculosis Vaccination Market comprises two key types: immune vaccines and therapy vaccines. Immune vaccines, such as the BCG vaccine, are designed to stimulate the body's immune response to prevent tuberculosis infection. In contrast, therapy vaccines aim to improve treatment outcomes for active TB cases by enhancing the immune response against the bacteria after infection. Both market types play crucial roles in controlling TB prevalence and advancing public health strategies to combat this infectious disease globally.</p></p>
