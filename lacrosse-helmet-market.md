<p><p>The lacrosse helmet market primarily features two materials: Expanded Polypropylene (EPP) and Double Density Vinyl Nitrile (VN). EPP is known for its lightweight properties and high energy absorption, making it ideal for impact resistance. Conversely, VN combines softness with durability, offering effective cushioning and comfort during play. These materials are integral to helmet design, ensuring player safety while enhancing performance. The choice between EPP and VN often depends on player preferences, safety standards, and specific league regulations.</p></p>
