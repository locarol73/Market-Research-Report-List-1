<p><p>Filtration fabrics are categorized into several types based on their weave structure. Monolayer weaves consist of a single layer, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for basic filtration needs. Double weaves provide enhanced strength and durability, making them suitable for more demanding applications. Three-layer weaves offer superior filtration through multiple layers, improving particle retention and flow rates. Other market types may include specialty fabrics designed for specific industrial uses, incorporating unique materials or structures to meet diverse filtration requirements.</p></p>
